iPhone Repair Warranty

iPhone Screen Repairs come with a 1 year warranty. Examples of what the warranty covers:

  • Blank Screen
  • Touch-screen not working correctly (e.g. dead spots)
  • Jagged lines
  • Flickering

Even if your repair was for replacing the iPhone Glass, and not the iPhone LCD, the warranty is still in effect as you get a new iPhone Screen with either repair.


The iPhone Screen Repair warranty is voided if there are any signs of physical damage to the screen or water damage to the phone or screen.

Warranty on Other Phone Repairs

All repiars other than iPhone screen repair have a 6 month warranty. The same exlusions apply.
