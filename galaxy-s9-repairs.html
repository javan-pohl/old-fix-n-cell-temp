Samsung Galaxy S9 Repairs & Screen Replacement

Galaxy S9 Screen Repair

Do you have a Galaxy S9 with a broken screen, cracked glass, or an S9 where the display suddenly went blank? FixNcell can get your broken S9 up and running again. FixNcell Phone Repair is a conveniently located cell phone repair shop in Westminster, Colorado, near the surrounding Broomfield and Arvada cities. Just a few minutes from both Denver and Boulder. Our technicians can fix the cracked screen on your Samsung Galaxy S9 and have it back to you in no time.

Galaxy S9 Screen Replacement

When we fix your cracked or damaged Samsung Galaxy S9 screen, we replace the entire screen assembly. Samsung Galaxy S9 screen replacement is the best process for fixing a cracked or damaged Galaxy S9 screen. With the curved face design of the Galaxy S9, it is no longer feasible to seperate the glass from the LCD (the Galaxy S9 actually comes with an AMOLED screen, not an LCD display) and replace simply the glass. Furthermore, with a full Galaxy S9 screen replacement, we also replace the metal frame assembly. So if the metal frame surrounding your Galaxy S9's AMOLED screen display was also damaged, your Samsung S9 will end up looking just like new after getting it fixed at FixNcell Phone Repair.

Black Dot on Galaxy S9 Screen

If the screen on your Galaxy S9 has a black dot on the image, this means the AMOLED display of your Galaxy S9 has been damaged. The dot may or may not spread to the rest of the screen, turning the entire S9 screen black. Fortunately, this issue is repaired with a Galaxy S9 screen replacement at FixNcell.

We fix all types of Galaxy S9 issues

In addition to replacing or repairing Galaxy S9 screens, we can also fix a wide variety of other malfunctions your Galaxy S9 might be having.

Galaxy S9 Not Charging?

If your Samsung Galaxy S9 is no longer charging, that may be an issue with the Galaxy S9 charging port or you may need to replace your battery. Fortunately, we here at FixNcell can repair your charging port and replace your Galaxy S9 battery.

Galaxy S9 "Temperature too low" error?

There can be several reasons why your Galaxy S9 is suddenly not charging and giving a "temperature is too low" error, but the main cause of this issue is that the wireless charging assembly of the Galaxy S9 has been damaged and needs to be replaced. Contact FixNcell today to inquire about fixing your broken wireless charging assembly and fixing the "temperature is too low" issue you're experiencing.
