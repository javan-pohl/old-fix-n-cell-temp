Types of iPhone 7 Repair at Fixncell

We offer a variety of iPhone 7 repairs. Here at FixNcell, we can replace your cracked iPhone 7 screen, replace damaged glass, fix a non-functioning touchscreen, or repair a display that is no longer showing an image.

How long does it take to repair an iPhone 7 Screen?

It takes about 30 minutes for us to complete an iPhone 7 screen repair.

Can you fix my iPhone 7 that's not charging?

There are three types of iPhone 7 charging issues: 1) damaged iPhone lightning port; 2) iPhone 7 battery needs to be replaced; 3) faulty iPhone uc charging chip. Sometimes it's hard to tell which of the three issue is actually preventing your iPhone 7 from charging. Luckily our technicians can diagnose the problem for you and have your iPhone 7 up and running again.

What if I don't know what kind of iPhone 7 Repair I need?

Our technicians can diagnose what the cause of your potential iPhone 7 functionality issue is. If it's not something we can repair, we don't charge a diagnostic fee. Usually, even if your iPhone 7 screen is blank or it doesn't want to boot up, we can have it up and running in no time. Most iPhone 7 repairs take about 30 minutes; though a proper diagnosis on your dead iPhone 7 might take additional time.

Do I need an appointment to get my iPhone 7 fixed?

Absolutely not. Appointments are not required to get your phone fixed at FixNcell Phone Repair. However, it can save you some and potentially money (you get $10 off your iPhone 7 Screen Repair). If you'd like to save $10 off your iPhone 7 screen repair, click here.

What's the difference between the iPhone 7 Plus and the iPhone 7?

The iPhone 7 is the smaller brother of the iPhone 7 Plus. The iPhone 7 has a metal back, unlike the newer iPhone 8, which has a glass back. If you're looking for information for repairs on the larger iPhone 7 Plus, click here.
