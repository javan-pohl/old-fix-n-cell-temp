iPhone 6s Plus Screen Repair Process

Either by scheduling an appointment or just walking in to the FixNcell Phone Repair store when you have time, you'll give your iPhone 6s Plus to our technician along with your name, phone number (or mention that you have an appointment), and passcode (so we can test the new iphone screen prior to install--you can also stay with us during your phone repair if you have privacy concerns).

Typically iPhone 6s Plus screen replacements take about 30 mintues but the iPhone Repair technician will inform you how long your iPhone 6s Plus Screen repair will take. If the screen replacement process will only take 30 minutes, you can wait around in store or come back at the designated time.

iPhone 6s Plus Screen Replacement Warranty

FixNcell Phone Repair's warranty on iPhone 6s Plus Screen Replacements is 12 months on functionality of the screen. Assuming there are no signs of physical damage to the iPhone 6s Plus screen that FixNcell Phone Repair installed (or signs of water damage to the internals of the phone), then your screen repair is still under its one year warranty. The iPhone 6s Plus Screen Repair warranty covers such issues as dead spots in the touchscreen, phantom touches (the iPhone screen pressing areas of the screen on its own), and a distored or blank image.

Types of Broken iPhone 6s Plus Screens

Whether your iPhone 6s Plus glass is competely shattered or the image on your iPhone 6s Plus screen is severly distored or totally blank, the issue will most likely be resolved by an iPhone 6s Plus Screen Repair. Your FixNcell iPhone Repair technician will inform you if there is a likelihood of anything else being damaged with your iPhone 6s Plus. If there is anything broken on your iPhone 6s Plus other than the screen, we will receive your explicit approval before proceeding with the repair. If we cannot repair your iPhone 6s Plus, we will not charge you.
