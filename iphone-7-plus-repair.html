iPhone 7 Plus Screen Repair Process

Schedule an appointment and a technician will come to your location to fix your iPhone 7 Plus on site. They do not need your password. Please make sure your phone has at least a 25% charge. The technician will perform the screen replacement in their vehicle but can do it in your home if you feel more comfortable with that. iPhone screen repair takes 15-30 minutes, barring any issues

iPhone 7 Plus Screen Replacement Warranty

FixNcell Phone Repair's warranty on iPhone 7 Plus Screen Replacements is 3 months on functionality of the screen. Assuming there are no signs of physical damage to the iPhone 7 Plus screen that FixNcell Phone Repair installed (or signs of water damage to the internals of the phone), then your screen repair is still under its three month warranty. The iPhone 7 Plus Screen Repair warranty covers such issues as dead spots in the touchscreen, phantom touches (the iPhone screen pressing areas of the screen on its own), and a distorted or blank image.

Types of Broken iPhone 7 Plus Screens

Whether your iPhone 7 Plus glass is completely shattered or the image on your iPhone 7 Plus screen is severely distorted or totally blank, the issue will most likely be resolved by an iPhone 7 Plus Screen Repair. Your FixNcell iPhone Repair technician will inform you if there is a likelihood of anything else being damaged with your iPhone 7 Plus. If there is anything broken on your iPhone 7 Plus other than the screen, we will receive your explicit approval before proceeding with the repair. If we cannot repair your iPhone 7 Plus, we will not charge you.
