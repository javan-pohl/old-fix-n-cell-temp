iPhone XR Repairs and Screen Replacement

We can fix broken screens and other issues on the iPhone Xr.

iPhone XR Screen Repair

If cracked glass on your iPhone Xr is bothering you or if you're having issues with the touchscreen or display, we can fix your broken iPhone Xr screen in under 30 minutes. Prices on replacing an iPhone Xr screen are declining every day so give FixNcell a call for the most recent information on repairing your damaged iPhone Xr screen.

iPhone XR No Image

If the display on your iPhone Xr has gone completely blank, but it still seems like the phone is on, it's most likely that the screen needs to be replaced. Oftentimes when an iPhone Xr LCD screen is damaged, the entire screen can go blank. Nothing too unusual about that and an easy thing to fix.

iPhone Xr Touchscreen Not Working

Sometimes when an iPhone Xr screen is cracked or damaged, you can start to experience issues with the touchscreen. The touchscreen's official name is the "digitizer" (coverting physical inputs into digital signals) and is often damaged when the iPhone Xr is damaged. In some cases the iPhone Xr touchscreen or digitizer can start to have issues even there is no obvious damage to the screen itself. Examples of touchscreen issues are dead-spots on the screen where touch does not work or "phantom presses" where the screen starts acting like areas of the screen are being touched when they are not. Having a screen repaired at FixNcell will fix any issues associated with your iPhone Xr touchscreen. If you have any issues with the touch after your iPhone Xr screen repair, our 12 month warranty will cover it as long as your new screen is not physically damaged.

iPhone Xr Not Charging

If you're having issues with getting your iPhone Xr to charge or to get the charging cable to fully insert into the iPhone Xr lightning port, your phone likely needs a charging port repair. FixNcell techs can replace your iPhone Xr charging port and have it charging again like new.

Broken iPhone Xr Camera

Whether your iPhone Xr camera has stopped working or the glass lens cover over the camera has cracked, FixNcell can repair it. Call us today for quotes on getting your iPhone Xr camera fixed.
