iPhone 5c Screen Repair Process

Either by scheduling an appointment or just walking in to the FixNcell Phone Repair store when you have time, you'll give your iPhone 5c to our technician along with your name, phone number (or mention that you have an appointment), and passcode (so we can test the new iphone screen prior to install--you can also stay with us during your phone repair if you have privacy concerns).

Typically iPhone 5c screen replacements take about 30 mintues but the iPhone Repair technician will inform you how long your iPhone 5c Screen repair will take. If the screen replacement process will only take 30 minutes, you can wait around in store or come back at the designated time.

iPhone 5c Screen Replacement Warranty

FixNcell Phone Repair's warranty on iPhone 5c Screen Replacements is 3 months on functionality of the screen. Assuming there are no signs of physical damage to the iPhone 5c screen that FixNcell Phone Repair installed (or signs of water damage to the internals of the phone), then your screen repair is still under its three month warranty. The iPhone 5c Screen Repair warranty covers such issues as dead spots in the touchscreen, phantom touches (the iPhone screen pressing areas of the screen on its own), and a distored or blank image.

Types of Broken iPhone 5c Screens

Whether your iPhone 5c glass is competely shattered or the image on your iPhone 5c screen is severly distored or totally blank, the issue will most likely be resolved by an iPhone 5c Screen Repair. Your FixNcell iPhone Repair technician will inform you if there is a likelihood of anything else being damaged with your iPhone 5c. If there is anything broken on your iPhone 5c other than the screen, we will receive your explicit approval before proceeding with the repair. If we cannot repair your iPhone 5c, we will not charge you.
