iPhone 7 Screen Repair Process

Either by scheduling an appointment or just walking in to the FixNcell Phone Repair store when you have time, you'll give your iPhone 7 to our technician along with your name, phone number (or mention that you have an appointment), and passcode (so we can test the new iphone screen prior to install--you can also stay with us during your phone repair if you have privacy concerns).

Typically iPhone 7 screen replacements take about 30 mintues but the iPhone Repair technician will inform you how long your iPhone 7 Screen repair will take. If the screen replacement process will only take 30 minutes, you can wait around in store or come back at the designated time.

iPhone 7 Screen Replacement Warranty

FixNcell Phone Repair's warranty on iPhone 7 Screen Replacements is 3 months on functionality of the screen. Assuming there are no signs of physical damage to the iPhone 7 screen that FixNcell Phone Repair installed (or signs of water damage to the internals of the phone), then your screen repair is still under its three month warranty. The iPhone 7 Screen Repair warranty covers such issues as dead spots in the touchscreen, phantom touches (the iPhone screen pressing areas of the screen on its own), and a distored or blank image.

Types of Broken iPhone 7 Screens

Whether your iPhone 7 glass is competely shattered or the image on your iPhone 7 screen is severly distored or totally blank, the issue will most likely be resolved by an iPhone 7 Screen Repair. Your FixNcell iPhone Repair technician will inform you if there is a likelihood of anything else being damaged with your iPhone 7. If there is anything broken on your iPhone 7 other than the screen, we will receive your explicit approval before proceeding with the repair. If we cannot repair your iPhone 7, we will not charge you.
