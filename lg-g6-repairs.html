How much does it cost to fix or replace an LG G6 screen?

Currently the price to repair an LG G6 screen at FixNcell is $119, or $109 if you schedule an appointment.

There's no image on my LG G6, will a screen replacement that issue?

Most likely. Sometimes when a phone screen is damaged, including on the LG G6 model, the display will go completely blank. At other times, you may see blank spots or fuzzy lines. Getting your LG G6 screen replaced will likely take care of those issues. We test all phone screens before installing them, where that's even a possibility (the LG G5 is one of those phones where we can't test the screen first, but the screen replacement process is straight-forward enough to where it's not an issue.)

The cracked glass on my LG G6 is sharp--is there anything I can do besides getting the screen repaired?

While repairing the screen on your LG G6 is still highly recommended, you can place packaging tape on the cracked glass to help prevent it from poking/stabbing/cutting you.
