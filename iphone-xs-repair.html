Now repairing iPhone XS screens. If you've only cracked the glass on your iPhone XS and the LCD/AMOLED is still showing the image on the screen like normal, give us a call to see if we can offer a lower price on your iPhone XS screen replacement.

iPhone XS Screen Replacement Process

The method we use at FixNcell Phone Repair to repair broken or cracked iPhone screens is to replace the entire screen assembly. So, when we fix your cracked iPhone XS screen, we replace the entire AMOLED screen assembly with a new one. If your AMOLED iPhone XS screen is still functioning normally without any flaws, we may be able to offer a bit of a discount on your iPhone XS screen repair. Call us for infos on getting your broken iPhone XS screen fixed. Additionally, if the your iPhone XS is blank, it could just be that it is in need of a new screen.

Why is iPhone XS screen repair more expensive than other iPhone screen repairs?

Traditionally, iPhone screens have utilized liquid crystal displays, or LCD. LCD screens have been easy to manufacture for decades. Starting with the iPhone XS, however, Apple has started to utilize the more expensive, better-looking, thinner, and more energy efficient AMOLED screens in their iPhones. Unfortunately AMOLED screens are pretty new to the industry and therefore still expensive to produce.
